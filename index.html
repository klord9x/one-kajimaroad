KAJIMA ROAD JAPAN

We take on the challenge of creating new value and contribute to the creation of a prosperous and comfortable environment for people and the earth.

Kajima Road is a Japanese road company founded in 1958 and has a history of 62 years. Furthermore, it is an affiliated company of Kajima Corporation, a major super general contractor. In addition to road construction, we also carry out runway paving work, waterproofing work, sports facilities, leisure facilities and other civil engineering and construction work.
